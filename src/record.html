BroShow Recorder (Firefox)

Recording window region

Firefox + macOS does not let extensions capture tab or window audio directly (Mozilla Bug 1541425, open since 2019). To record Firefox's audio without a microphone, install BlackHole (free, open source) and follow the multi-output setup guide. Then check this box and Firefox's audio will be picked up as if it were a microphone.
