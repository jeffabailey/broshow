# JTBD Job Stories: browser-tab-recorder

## Primary Job

**When** I need to capture what's happening in a browser tab,
**I want to** click one button and get an mp4 file of just that tab,
**so I can** archive it without fussing with complex screen recording tools.

### Dimensions
- **Functional**: Record a single browser tab's audio+video and save as mp4
- **Emotional**: Feel in control — no anxiety about what else is being captured
- **Social**: N/A (archival use case — not primarily for sharing)

## Secondary Jobs

### Job 2: Tutorial/Demo Creation
**When** I'm creating a walkthrough of a web application,
**I want to** record just the tab content in a universally compatible format,
**so I can** produce clean demos without desktop clutter leaking in.

### Job 3: Capturing Live Content
**When** a stream, webinar, or presentation is happening in my browser,
**I want to** start recording with minimal setup,
**so I can** save it before it disappears.

### Job 4: Bug Reporting
**When** I encounter unexpected browser behavior,
**I want to** quickly capture a recording of the tab,
**so I can** attach it to a bug report without explaining in words.

## Job Hierarchy

| Priority | Job | Frequency | Importance |
|----------|-----|-----------|------------|
| 1 | One-click tab recording to mp4 | High | Critical |
| 2 | Tutorial/demo capture | Medium | High |
| 3 | Live content capture | Medium | Medium |
| 4 | Bug reporting | Low-Medium | Medium |
